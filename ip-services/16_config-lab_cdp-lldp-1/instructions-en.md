# CDP and LLDP

## Objectives

Configure and verify Cisco Discovery Protocol (CDP) and Link Layer Discovery Protocol (LLDP) for network discovery and documentation.

## Instructions

1. Enable or disable CDP globally and per-interface.
2. Enable LLDP globally and per-interface.
3. Use CDP and LLDP to discover neighboring devices.
4. Document neighbor information including device ID, IP address, and platform.

## Verification

- Use `show cdp neighbors` and `show cdp neighbors detail` for CDP.
- Use `show lldp neighbors` and `show lldp neighbors detail` for LLDP.
