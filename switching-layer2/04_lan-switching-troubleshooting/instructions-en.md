# LAN Switching Troubleshooting

## Objectives

Diagnose and resolve common Layer 2 switching issues including VLAN mismatches, trunk problems, and STP errors.

## Instructions

1. Verify VLAN assignments on all switches.
2. Check trunk configurations and allowed VLANs.
3. Examine STP status and port roles.
4. Verify MAC address tables.
5. Identify and correct any Layer 2 issues.

## Verification

- Use `show vlan brief`.
- Use `show interfaces trunk`.
- Use `show spanning-tree`.
- Use `show mac address-table`.
